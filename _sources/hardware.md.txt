## Electronics Details

The Fleshlight Launch circuit boards uses:

- [Microchip PIC24FJ64 Processor](http://www.microchip.com/wwwproducts/en/PIC24FJ64GA004)
- [Microship RN4871 Bluetooth Radio](http://www.microchip.com/wwwproducts/en/RN4871)
- [ST Electronics VNS5180A HBridge](http://www.st.com/en/automotive-analog-power-ics/vnh5180a-e.html)
